Privacy Policy

Everlast Tracker  ·  Last updated: June 9, 2026

Everlast Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use the app:

  • Account information: Email address and username, collected when you create an account via Firebase Authentication.
  • Workout data: Punch counts, session duration, power scores, and E.V.E.R. Score history generated during your training sessions.
  • Sensor data: Accelerometer data transmitted from your Everlast Bluetooth punch sensor during active sessions. This data is processed on-device and summarized results are stored in your account.
  • Profile information: Display name and optional avatar image you choose to add.
  • Community activity: Fist bumps you give or receive, and content you post in community feeds.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality including session tracking, performance analytics, and leaderboards.
  • To display your profile and activity to other users in community features (only information you choose to share publicly).
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs using aggregated, non-identifiable usage data.
  • To communicate with you about your account if necessary.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. Firebase's security and privacy practices are described at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • Service providers: Google Firebase (data storage and authentication), and video hosting infrastructure used to deliver workout content.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Community Features

When you participate in community features (feed posts, fist bumps, leaderboards), your username and activity may be visible to other users of the app. You can adjust your profile visibility in Settings at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

Everlast Tracker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and correction: You can view and update your profile information at any time within the app.
  • Account deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below. We will process your request within 30 days.
  • Data portability: You may request a copy of your workout data by contacting us.

8. Bluetooth and Device Permissions

The app requests Bluetooth access solely to connect to your Everlast punch sensor. We do not use Bluetooth to track your location or collect any data from other nearby devices.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
